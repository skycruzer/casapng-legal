Privacy Policy

SkyRegs PNG is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

The App stores your preferences (appearance, favorites, recent documents) locally on your device using UserDefaults. No personal data is sent to our servers.

Documents

PDF documents are downloaded from our Supabase storage and cached locally on your device for offline access. These are publicly available CASA PNG aviation regulations.

Analytics

The App does not include any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services:

  • Supabase — for document storage and delivery
  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription management

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.
